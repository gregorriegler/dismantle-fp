Kata: Data Munging
------------------

Part One: Weather Data: In weather.dat you'll find daily weather data for 
Morristown, NJ for June 2002. Write a program to output the day number 
(column one) with the smallest temperature spread (the maximum temperature 
is the second column, the minimum the third column).

See http://codekata.com/kata/kata04-data-munging/
